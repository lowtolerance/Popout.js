Bakery

Our bakery features a wide assortment of breads, rolls, cakes and pies. Our bakers are passionate about their work, and they love to bake something new every day, so you never know what savory and sweet delights you will stumble across. We sell our cakes and pies whole or by the slice.

We also do custom bakes, so if there is a particular type of dessert, pastry or bread that you are looking for, give us advance notice and we will do our best to meet your needs.

Our bakery is open from 8AM to 5PM, Monday through Saturday, but you can purchase any of our baked goods or desserts in the restaurant!
