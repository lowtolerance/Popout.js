About Us

Generica is an imaginary vegetarian/vegan restaurant. We believe in supporting our local community, and as such, we offer only the freshest produce, sourced directly from local farms.

Our walls feature works of art by local artisans and craftsmen, which are available for purchase, with 100% of the proceeds going to the maker. In addition to our cuisine, we also offer a full bar, and an extensive wine collection.

Currently, we are featuring an Autumn menu, but our menu changes seasonally. We also offer many daily specials, as we like to provide our patrons with a new experience with each visit.

Generica is inspired by Ipanema, a vegetarian restaurant on Grace Street in Richmond, Virginia.
